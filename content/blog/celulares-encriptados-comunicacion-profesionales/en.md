## What is an encrypted phone?

An encrypted phone is a mobile device that integrates advanced encryption and digital security technologies to protect calls, messages, files, and network connections.

Unlike a conventional smartphone, these devices prioritize information protection and user privacy through mechanisms such as:

- End-to-end encryption
- Hardened operating systems
- Protection against communication interception
- Advanced access control
- Secure data deletion

These features make encrypted phones essential tools for professionals who require secure and private communications.
