## How does Silent Phone work?

Silent Phone combines multiple security technologies designed to protect both the content of communications and the information surrounding them.

### End-to-end encryption

The foundation of Silent Phone's security is end-to-end encryption.

This means:

- Messages are encrypted directly on the sender's device.
- They remain protected throughout transmission.
- Only the intended recipient can decrypt and read them.

Neither internet service providers, mobile carriers, nor third parties can access the content of the communication.
